Event Production & AV Project Manager · Hawaii

I started at the board — mixing consoles, signal flow, live sound for churches and venues. I trained at FIRST Institute in Recording Arts and Show Production, graduating valedictorian in 2019, and went straight into live production work in Orlando and Savannah.

As a US Army veteran, I brought that same discipline into a contracting opportunity in Baghdad supporting AV and communications systems for CENTCOM — and later at US Pacific Command in Pearl Harbor. When COVID hit, I also kept the audio side going — running front-of-house for congregations pivoting to online services and helping launch online worship experiences for 10 churches during the pandemic, building real hybrid and livestream production experience in the process.

Acoustic Aura is where both sides come together. Audio is what I know. Production is what I do. Hawaii is where I'm building it.
